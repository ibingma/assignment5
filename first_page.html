About me

In my current role as a Produce Quality Inspector for a chain of grocery stores, I leverage my extensive background in farming, food science, and production management to ensure that only the highest quality produce reaches our customers. My responsibilities involve meticulously inspecting all incoming produce at the distribution warehouse, assessing factors such as freshness, ripeness, and overall quality before it is distributed to the stores.

This position aligns perfectly with my career journey, from my early interest in gardening to my formal education in farming and food science at Durham College. The skills I honed during my time in the greenhouse and fields, combined with my experience in managing production processes, have equipped me to excel in this role. My passion for horticulture and commitment to quality assurance drive me to uphold the highest standards in produce inspection, contributing to the success of the grocery chain and the satisfaction of its customers.
